Text: 

Lovely little animated film 'Designed by Apple in California'. A great example of how to bring a message and story to life simply. Everything about this animation compliments the philosophy we have come to know and expect from Apple. Even down to the perfectly timed and appropriate sound effects for each transition or movement. A story designed by Apple which tells the tale of what designers do.
